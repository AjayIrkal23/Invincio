FAQ

  • Teams need to participate in all events to qualify for General Championship (GC) Each team will have a maximum of 10 member.
  • Registration Fees: Rs. 2000/- per team for outstation teams, which includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner and Rs. 1500/- for local teams. The college can send multiple teams. However, the members should not be repeated in other teams.
  • Accommodation facility will be provided to the outstation teams only from 22nd July to 23rd July 2022 from 7.00 AM to 7.00 AM. (24 hrs)
  • All students who are attending the event should carry an Aadhar Card or Voter ID as identity proof as well as their College ID cards.
  • Payment Mode: UPI QR Code.(Which is present in Registration form).
  • Reporting Time for the Event: 22nd July 2022 at 8:00 AM sharp.
  • Last date of Registration: 18th July 2022.
